Adout Ether

The world famous Ether Modeling School was founded in 1969 on Fifth Avenue in New York. We have been in forefront of the modeling industry for over 50 years.

Since its opening, our agency has been the industry leader in placing Women, Men, Kids and Teens all over the world and helping them to fulfill their dreams about the runway. We work with world-known brands and may be your first step on your way to celebrity and fame.

FAQ

We're always looking for new faces and talented women, men, teens and kids wanting to start their career in modeling. If you've always dreamed about a runway, Ether is your chance!

You will find the answers to the frequently asked questions. If you have another one, just contact us.

HOW TO BECOME A MODEL?

Sign up for one of our courses or attend our summer camp. Next, order a portfolio and find an agent.

CAN A CHILD BECOME A MODEL?

Yes, sure. Moreover, often children perceive modeling courses more as a game and get great pleasure.

WHAT SIZE SHOULD I WEAR TO BECOME A MODEL?

In the modern world, there are no such strict requirements for the size of clothes or physical parameters.

CAN YOU HELP TO SIGN MY FIRST CONTRACT?

Yes, while studying at our school, each model enters into his or her first contract with an advertising agency or brand.
